# EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

## Company Profile Overview

**Alumimundo S.A.** is Costa Rica's premier distributor and advisor for high-end construction finishes, fixtures, and hardware, with over 39 years of experience (established 1985). Operating from two state-of-the-art showrooms in San José (Pavas Industrial Zone) and Guanacaste (Solarium development near Liberia International Airport), they serve as the exclusive distributor for KOHLER and represent global brands including Schlage, Steelcraft, and Kallista's luxury line.

**Core Business Model:** Hybrid distributor-advisor model combining:
- **Product Distribution**: High-end bathroom/kitchen fixtures, doors, hardware, ceilings, flooring, facades, lighting, and furniture
- **Specification Services**: Technical advisory for architects, designers, and developers
- **Professional Training**: Factory-certified training programs on seismic codes, fire safety, and maintenance standards
- **Project Support**: End-to-end support from design specification through installation

## Risk Assessment & Mitigation

### Technical Risks

**High Risk:**
- **Data Quality**: Product data may be incomplete, inconsistent, or in multiple formats
  - *Mitigation*: Phase 1 data cleaning and standardization project; establish data governance

**Medium Risk:**
- **Integration Complexity**: Legacy systems may not have APIs
  - *Mitigation*: Build middleware layer; use RPA for system-to-system data transfer if needed

**Low Risk:**
- **AI Model Performance**: Models may not meet accuracy requirements initially
  - *Mitigation*: Start with simpler models; establish human-in-the-loop workflows; continuous retraining

### Operational Risks

**High Risk:**
- **Change Management**: Staff resistance to AI-augmented workflows
  - *Mitigation*: Co-design workshops; early wins focus; transparent communication; reframe as "augmentation not replacement"

**Medium Risk:**
- **Training Requirements**: Staff may need significant upskilling
  - *Mitigation*: Phased rollout with comprehensive training; hire AI champion within organization

### Market Risks

**Medium Risk:**
- **Customer Adoption**: Architects/designers may prefer traditional human interaction
  - *Mitigation*: Hybrid model maintaining personal relationships; position AI as tool that enhances rather than replaces advisors

**Low Risk:**
- **Competitive Response**: Competitors may rapidly copy AI features
  - *Mitigation*: Data moat and network effects create defensibility; continuous innovation
